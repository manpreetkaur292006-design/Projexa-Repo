Titan

Titan Company Limited is a leading Indian watch and lifestyle company founded in 1984 as a joint venture between the Tata Group and the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO). It is headquartered in Bengaluru, India, and became famous for transforming the Indian watch industry with stylish, affordable, and high-quality timepieces. Titan introduced modern quartz technology and made watches a fashion accessory for Indian consumers. The company operates popular brands such as Titan, Fastrack, and Sonata and is also known for its strong presence in jewelry through Tanishq. Titan watches are recognized for their reliability, elegant designs, and innovation in both analog and smartwatches. Over the years, Titan has earned the trust of millions of customers and is regarded as one of India's most respected and successful watch brands. .

TITAN

Titan Company Limited is a leading Indian lifestyle and luxury watch brand founded in 1984 as a joint venture between the Tata Group and the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO). Headquartered in Bengaluru, India, Titan revolutionized the Indian watch industry by introducing quartz technology and stylish, affordable timepieces. The company became well known for its innovation, quality, and wide range of designs that cater to different age groups and lifestyles. Titan operates as a trusted and independent brand under the Tata Group and follows high standards of manufacturing and precision. Its popular collections include Titan Edge, Fastrack, Sonata, and Raga. Titan watches have been worn by millions of customers across India and are associated with reliability and modern design. Today, Titan is considered one of India’s most respected and successful watch brands with a strong presence in both domestic and international markets..
