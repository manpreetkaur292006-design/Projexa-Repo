Patek Philippe

Patek Philippe is a prestigious Swiss luxury watch company founded in 1839 in Geneva by Antoni Patek and Adrien Philippe. It became famous for inventing the keyless winding mechanism and for its many innovations in mechanical watchmaking. The company is family-owned by the Stern family since 1932 and remains independent. Patek Philippe watches are handcrafted with extreme precision and can take months or even years to produce. The brand is known for complex “complications” like perpetual calendars and minute repeaters. Its watches have been owned by famous personalities such as Queen Victoria and Albert Einstein. Today, Patek Philippe is considered one of the most respected and valuable watch brands in the world.

ROLEX

Rolex is a world-renowned Swiss luxury watch company founded in 1905 in London by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis, and later headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland. It became famous for creating the first waterproof wristwatch, the Oyster, and for its major innovations in precision and durability. Rolex is owned by the Hans Wilsdorf Foundation and operates as an independent company. Rolex watches are manufactured with exceptional craftsmanship and undergo rigorous testing for accuracy and reliability. The brand is known for iconic models such as the Submariner, Daytona, and Datejust. Its watches have been worn by famous personalities including Sir Edmund Hillary and James Bond. Today, Rolex is considered one of the most respected and valuable luxury watch brands in the world..
